AIRYL

AIRYL, or AI Reads Your Letters, was one of my final year projects on AI. It is a chatbot written for Android devices in AIML.

AIML is an XML-based markup language meant to act like an AI replying to responses. However, the responses can be tailored to suit personal needs. You may look up AIML here. Check out the chatbot here.
